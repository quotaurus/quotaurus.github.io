RevFlow product notes and roadmap.

A lightweight public record of current RevFlow capabilities and direction. Roadmap notes are directional and not a delivery commitment.

Website refresh - 10 July 2026

Public site updated so Quotaurus is the company and RevFlow is the Jira app. The new site focuses on quote-to-delivery revenue control, setup, reporting, dark mode compatibility, and Marketplace buyer confidence.

RevFlow 0.2.0 productisation pass

  • Project-level setup state and first-run setup banner.
  • Guided setup wizard for Jira fields, issue types, statuses, status buckets, company details, currency, tax, quote items, clients, revenue streams, features, email, and access.
  • Configurable permissions for setup, quote actions, deletion, sending, and report viewing.
  • Mailgun and SendGrid provider options for optional quote email sending.
  • Billing export columns and setup import/export.
  • Reporting logic that honours configured status and issue-type mappings.
  • Missing quote coverage reports for sprint work.
  • Jira dark mode compatibility through Forge theme support and dedicated styling.

Current app workflow

  • Jira project page and issue panel named RevFlow.
  • Quote creation, issue linking, line items, PDFs, statuses, and version history.
  • Saved quote filtering by status, system, created date, and search text.
  • Quote funnel, value over time, top clients, systems, revenue streams, billable activity, and completion-month reports.
  • Recurring monthly quote templates, including last-day-of-month schedules.
