Use cases

Quote work that starts in Jira.

Quotaurus is strongest when the thing being quoted already has Jira context: a ticket, project, change request, support package, or forecasted delivery date.

Digital agencies

Turn client requests, change tickets, and project scope into priced quotes without copying details into a spreadsheet first.

  • Quote new project work from Jira issues.
  • Keep accepted value visible beside delivery scope.
  • Track quote versions when scope changes.

IT service teams and MSPs

Quote one-off work and monthly service packages while keeping delivery tickets, recurring drafts, and sent quote records together.

  • Create recurring monthly quote drafts from templates.
  • Use PO, system, revenue stream, and forecast date fields.
  • Generate PDFs without building them manually.

Professional services

Capture the commercial details that usually drift across documents, email threads, and finance spreadsheets.

  • Use clients, products, rates, quantity, VAT, and currency.
  • Track Draft, Sent, Accepted, Rejected, and Invoiceable status.
  • Keep forecasted completion dates near quote value.

Jira admins

Add a focused quote workflow for Jira Cloud teams without rolling out a separate CPQ platform. Quotaurus runs inside Jira project pages and issue panels, and installation is handled through the Atlassian Marketplace route.

Best fit

Quotaurus is a good fit when quotes need to stay close to delivery work, quote PDFs matter, and recurring monthly drafts are part of the team's workflow.
