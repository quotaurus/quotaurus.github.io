Quote app for Jira Cloud

Quote from the work already in Jira.

Quotaurus gives teams a Jira-native way to build priced quotes, connect them to issues, generate PDFs, send quote emails when configured, and keep quote status history close to delivery work.

Install from Marketplace Ask a question

Why teams use it

Commercial context stays with delivery context.

When quote details live separately from Jira, teams lose the link between scope, price, client details, sent documents, and recurring monthly work. Quotaurus keeps the quote record beside the project and issues it belongs to.

Benefits

Everything needed to quote from Jira work.

01

Create quotes from Jira issues

Start a quote from the issue panel or project Quotes page, so pricing begins from the ticket context your team already has.

02

Keep quotes tied to scope

Link open quotes to Jira issues, see linked quote references from the issue panel, and jump back to the full quote in the project page.

03

Build structured pricing

Capture clients, products, rates, quantities, VAT, currency, revenue streams, systems, PO numbers, and forecast dates without leaving Jira.

04

Send polished quote PDFs

Preview a generated PDF and send it by email when Mailgun is configured, with recipient and sent-date details stored on the quote.

05

Track quote progress

Use Draft, Sent, Accepted, Rejected, Invoiceable, Closed, and Cancelled statuses, with version history available for quote updates.

06

Repeat monthly quote work

Set up recurring quote templates that create monthly drafts on the selected billing day, including last-day-of-month schedules.

How it fits

From Jira ticket to quote record.

  1. Create Start from a ticket or the project Quotes page.
  2. Price Add client, product, VAT, currency, PO, and forecast detail.
