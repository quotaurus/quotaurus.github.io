Security & Trust

Know what the app stores and where data flows.

Quotaurus handles commercial quote data, so the site documents the current data model, Jira scopes, external email dependency, and support contacts before installation.

App hosting

Quotaurus is built as an Atlassian Forge app for Jira Cloud. The app exposes a Jira project page named Quotes and a Jira issue panel named Quotes.

App storage

Quote records, clients, metadata, recurring templates, history, and settings are stored through Atlassian Forge app storage APIs used by the app.

Website cookies

The public website does not currently set analytics cookies, advertising cookies, account registration cookies, or contact-form cookies.

Requested Jira scopes

  • storage:app for app data storage.
  • read:jira-work to read Jira work context.
  • write:jira-work to support Jira work interactions used by the app.
  • read:jira-user to read Jira user details where needed, such as prepared-by information.

External email flow

Quote email sending uses Mailgun when configured. The quote email flow sends recipient details, email content, and the generated quote PDF to the configured Mailgun endpoint.

Quote data processed by the app

Quotaurus may process client names, recipient names and emails, quote titles, quote values, products, rates, quantities, VAT settings, currency, PO numbers, systems, revenue streams, notes, forecasted completion dates, linked Jira issue keys, generated PDF content, quote status, version history, recurring templates, and sent-email metadata.

Data outside Atlassian

The core quote workflow is stored through Forge app storage. When Mailgun sending is configured and a user sends a quote email, quote email content and the PDF attachment are sent to Mailgun for delivery. Support emails sent to Quotaurus are processed by the email provider used for the relevant support mailbox.

Data retention and deletion

The public site does not publish an automated retention schedule. Contact Quotaurus for access, correction, deletion, or uninstall data questions. Include the Atlassian site URL and enough detail to locate the relevant records.

Backups and incident response

Backup, disaster recovery, and incident response details should be confirmed during procurement or Marketplace security review. Security reports can be sent to support@quotaurus.com.

Subprocessors

Atlassian provides the Jira Cloud and Forge platform used by the app. Mailgun is involved only when quote email sending is configured and used. Additional support-mailbox providers may process support messages sent by email.

Security contact

For vulnerability reports, email support@quotaurus.com with a clear subject line. Avoid public disclosure until the issue has been reviewed.
